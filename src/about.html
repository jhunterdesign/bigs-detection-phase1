Our Story

From a backyard smoke pit to serving Metro Atlanta.

How It All Began

Big’s BBQ started behind a small house in Marietta, Georgia with nothing more than a homemade smoker, a family recipe, and neighbors who kept showing up asking, “What you cooking today?”

What began as weekend cookouts for friends and church gatherings slowly turned into something bigger. Word spread. Plates multiplied. And before long, the backyard smoke pit became the heart of a community tradition.

Meet Big

Known simply as “Big” to friends and family, the founder of Big’s BBQ learned the craft from his father and grandfather, who believed that good barbecue takes time, patience, and love.

Big never rushed the process. Meats were smoked low and slow. Sauces were made from scratch. And every plate was served like it was going to a family member.

Why BBQ?

For Big, barbecue wasn’t just food — it was fellowship. It was the reason people gathered, laughed, and stayed a little longer. It was a way to bring people together across neighborhoods and generations.

From Backyard to Metro Atlanta

Today, Big’s BBQ & Smokehouse proudly serves guests from Atlanta, Decatur, and Marietta. While the setting has grown, the method hasn’t changed. Every brisket, rack of ribs, and side dish is still prepared the same way it was in that backyard years ago.

Slow. Careful. And made to be shared.
